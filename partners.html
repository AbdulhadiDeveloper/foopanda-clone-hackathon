How does it work?

1

The customer adds their delivery address in the app or on the website and then selects a store or restaurant in their area.

2

You accept the order with the reading tablet and prepare it for the collection time.

3

A rider will come to you at the specified time and collect the order. It is then delivered directly to the customer!

4

Twice per month you receive payments for the orders you have received. You automatically receive a basis for the deposit and a detailed insight into your sales statistics.

How can I receive orders?

You will receive orders through the foodpanda merchant tablet provided to you. Simply accept the order, prepare the food, and a rider will be there to pick it up.

Are there any costs if I register on the site?

Registration is free. Commission fees only apply to successful orders processed through the platform. Our team will discuss the specific rates during your onboarding.

What are the opening hours like?

You have full control over your opening hours. You can set your regular schedule or temporarily pause orders during busy times directly from the merchant portal.
